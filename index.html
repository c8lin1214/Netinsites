Services

Consulting

At NetInsites we know that using the Internet effectively is more than using a Designer to put together a Website. Your Internet strategy must be integrated within your business planning process and should ensure that you communicate more successfully with all your stakeholders

E-Commerce

Your e-commerce site will be based on OpenCart an open source e-commerce system. We have modified and improved the OpenCart system. You will need to choose one of the available OpenCart templates to set the structure of the site. The idea is to make the selling of your products the focus of the site.

Web Development

Before we build a Website we look closely at the strategy behind the proposal and make sure it fits with the proposed design and structure. Although we use the latest Internet technology and keep up with the latest trends the effectiveness of the Website always comes first. If your business isn't improved by initiating or upgrading your Web presence then the project is a failure.

Staff

Alex Garden

Alex has an MBA (with distinction) from Massey University that he completed in 1997, and an earlier degree, a BSurv (Bachelor of Land Surveying) from the University of Otago (1982). On completion of his MBA Alex spent a year in Vancouver, Canada studying Internet trends before returning to New Zealand to start NetInsites.

Before that he was involved in a number of industries including professional services, construction, manufacturing and telecommunications. Alex has always worked in the small business arena and has spent time at the head of a manufacturing company.

As well as designing Websites and helping companies with their Internet strategies, Alex makes himself available for presentations and has written for a number of publications and he is also frequently quoted in articles about the Internet.

Natalie Pike

Natalie joined Netinsites in March 2009 after completing a Diploma of Web Develpment. Before that she was involved in the printing industry for 7 years. Her background within this industry consisted of administration, accounts, business development, sales and customer services.

Natalie developed her interest in Web development and design after enrolling in a 'taster' course at a design school and from there she went on to complete her Diploma.
